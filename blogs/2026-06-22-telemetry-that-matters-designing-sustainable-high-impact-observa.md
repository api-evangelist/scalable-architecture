As system architectures grow more complex, cloud-native teams are drowning in their own telemetry data. The article shows how to design observability pipelines that focus on meaningful signals rather than collecting everything, introduces the "observability mesh" concept connecting traces, metrics, logs, and profiles, and weighs automatic versus manual instrumentation strategies.
