The CARE Program now lets passing or recertifying the Certified Kubernetes Security Specialist (CKS) exam automatically reinstate or extend your Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA) certification. The change recognizes the skill progression from administration into security and simplifies renewal for professionals holding multiple credentials.
