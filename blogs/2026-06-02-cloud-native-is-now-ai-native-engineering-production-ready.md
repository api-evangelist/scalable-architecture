CNCF examines how cloud native infrastructure has evolved to become the foundation for production-ready AI workloads, arguing that "cloud native" and "AI-native" are now inseparable. The post covers how Kubernetes, service meshes, and other CNCF projects are being adapted and extended to handle the demands of large-scale AI model training and inference. Teams building AI systems at scale are encouraged to leverage existing cloud native patterns and tooling.
